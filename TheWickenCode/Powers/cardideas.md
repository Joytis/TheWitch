# Card / Mechanic Ideas (parking lot)

## The Cauldron — steal enemy power as brewing fuel

Shelved variant of the Hex "stolen power" design. Instead of the stolen power going to
the witch (which became Hex proper), the enemy's power is **harvested as an ingredient** —
their strength/block/buffs convert into brewing/potion fuel.

- Flavor: the witch bottles the enemy's essence as a reagent. "Bottle their strength."
- Ties the **Potion** pillar rather than the Familiar/self pillar.
- Open questions: what's the fuel unit (a resource counter? a token potion?), how it feeds
  brewing/upgrade, and whether it's a debuff-on-enemy or a standalone card/relic effect.
- Not Hex — Hex is locked as "steal power at the end of an enemy's turn" (power flows to the
  witch/coven). This is a separate Potion-pillar concept worth revisiting later.
