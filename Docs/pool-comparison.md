## Method & caveats

- Static numbers catch **outliers and shape mismatches**, not true balance: synergy, energy cheat, upgrade access, and potion output (a Witch identity!) are invisible here.
- Flat-bucket medians only; scaling/X/conditional cards are counted, never averaged.
- Base-game JSON text omits keywords (Sly/Exhaust/Innate/Retain/star costs) — see `taxonomy.md` data-quality notes; `threads[]` carries the ground truth used for the downside column.
- Multi-hit parsed from card text ("N times"/"twice", same sentence as the damage clause). Conditional bonus hits count as 1 hit.
- Witch damage also arrives via potions and familiars (tokens) — cross-check §7 and the potion system before reading §2 as the whole damage story.
